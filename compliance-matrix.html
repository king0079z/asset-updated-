+ Additional Built-In Application Features

The AssetXAI platform includes the following additional features and modules beyond the mandatory RFQ requirements — all fully built, operational, and included in the solution at no extra cost. These extend the platform's value and capabilities for QRCS.

⭐ Full Application Customisation — Up to 15% Free of Charge FREE OF CHARGE
AssetXAI provides comprehensive customisation of up to 15% of total system scope at no additional cost. This covers: QRCS branding (logo, colour scheme, Arabic/English RTL layout), custom workflows matching QRCS organisational procedures, bespoke field additions, custom role hierarchies, and custom report templates. Any additional features requested beyond the base scope are provided free of charge within this envelope — so QRCS always gets exactly what is needed.
Vehicle Fleet Management Built-In
Complete vehicle fleet management: add/edit/delete vehicles (car, truck, van, bus, motorcycle), track status (available/rented/maintenance), insurance and registration expiry tracking, vehicle image management, and fleet overview dashboard with KPIs.
Real-Time GPS Vehicle Tracking Built-In
Live GPS tracking for all fleet vehicles via OBD and GPS tracker devices. Real-time map view showing vehicle positions, speed, and heading. Trip history playback, geofencing alerts, and device location history with millisecond-precision timestamps.
Automatic Trip Detection & Management Built-In
AI-powered automatic trip start/end detection based on movement patterns. Manual trip management with route recording, start/end coordinates, distance calculation, and trip analytics. Offline trip monitoring syncs automatically when connectivity restored.
Driver Management & Assignment Built-In
Driver profiles with licence details, assignment to vehicles, trip history per driver, driver performance analytics, and driver route optimisation using AI. Driver trip summary reports with distance, duration, and activity logs.
Vehicle Rental Management Built-In
Full vehicle rental workflow: rental agreements, daily/total cost calculations, rental history, cost reporting per vehicle and per user. Rental status tracking (active/completed) with automated cost summaries and rental performance dashboards.
Vehicle Maintenance & Service History Built-In
Vehicle maintenance scheduling with service type, cost, mileage, vendor assignment, and next due date. AI maintenance recommendations based on usage patterns. Full maintenance history per vehicle with receipt upload and service records.
Multi-Kitchen Food Supply Management Built-In
Comprehensive food supply inventory management across multiple kitchens and locations. Tracks quantity, unit, expiry dates, supplier, cost per unit, and storage location. Supports batch numbers, barcode scanning, and bulk operations. Ideal for humanitarian field kitchens.
Recipe Management with Cost Calculation Built-In
Recipe library with ingredient management, automatic cost calculation, selling price and profit analysis, sub-recipe support, servings management, and recipe barcodes. Recipe usage tracking by kitchen with consumption cost recording and kitchen breakdown analytics.
AI-Powered Food Consumption Analysis Built-In
Machine learning analysis of food consumption patterns across kitchens. Identifies anomalies, predicts demand, generates AI-driven procurement recommendations, and provides detailed consumption breakdowns by ingredient, recipe, and time period.
Food Waste & Disposal Tracking Built-In
Detailed food waste tracking by reason, quantity, cost, and kitchen. Waste pattern analytics with trend identification, waste reduction recommendations, ingredient waste analysis, and waste history reports. Supports humanitarian reporting on food utilisation efficiency.
Food Demand Forecasting Built-In
AI-based food demand forecasting per kitchen using historical consumption data. Generates procurement recommendations, identifies overpurchasing by location, calculates lead times, and provides inventory replenishment alerts to prevent stockouts.
Kitchen Financial Metrics & Analytics Built-In
Full financial performance per kitchen: monthly consumption value, expense tracking, cost per serving, budget vs actual analysis, kitchen comparison dashboards, and management reporting. Exportable financial summaries for organisational budgeting.
Multi-Location Inventory & Stock Transfers Built-In
Centralised inventory tracking across all QRCS locations. Stock transfer workflows between locations with approval, item-level transfer tracking, location inventory dashboards, and overpurchasing analysis per site to optimise distribution.
Purchase Orders & Production Batches Built-In
Purchase order management with vendor assignment, item-level ordering, and approval workflow. Production batch tracking for bulk preparation with status (planned/in-progress/completed), linked recipes, and output quantity recording.
AI-Powered Enterprise Dashboard Built-In
Intelligent executive dashboard with real-time KPIs across all modules: asset health scores, ticket resolution rates, RFID coverage, food supply status, vehicle utilisation, and staff performance. AI-generated insights with natural language summaries and actionable recommendations.
AI Insights & ML Predictions Built-In
Comprehensive ML analytics layer: asset failure probability prediction, maintenance cost forecasting, inventory demand prediction, anomaly detection in consumption patterns, driver behaviour analysis, and automated root cause identification for recurring issues.
AI-Suggested Task Planning Built-In
AI engine that automatically suggests maintenance tasks, inspection schedules, and preventive actions based on asset health scores, historical patterns, and upcoming expirations. Tasks are auto-assigned to available technicians with estimated effort and priority scores.
Vendor Performance Management Built-In
Comprehensive vendor evaluation with quantitative scoring across reliability, quality, and response time dimensions. Performance trend tracking, KPI dashboards, evaluation workflows, automated performance reminders, and vendor comparison reports for procurement decision support.
3D Interactive Floor Map Visualisation Built-In
3D floor plan visualisation with real-time asset overlays. Upload custom SVG or CAD floor plans, define RFID zones graphically via drag-and-drop zone editor, and view asset locations animated in real time. Supports multi-floor, multi-building layouts.
Asset Flow Path & Area Distribution Statistics Built-In
Visual analytics of asset movement paths across zones with dwell-time analysis and circulation statistics. Area distribution heatmaps show density across the floor map. Exportable flow reports for operational efficiency analysis and zone capacity planning.
RFID Room Tag Printing & Zone Mapping Built-In
Generate and print RFID room tags for physical zone identification. Room tag printer dialog with barcode/QR generation per zone. Zone map editor with visual drag-and-drop positioning, AP MAC address binding, and restricted zone configuration.
Advanced Print & PDF Reports Built-In
Professional print-ready reports across all modules: asset reports, ticket summaries, kitchen consumption reports, food supply reports, audit reports, RFID movement reports, and compliance matrices. All reports are PDF-exportable directly from the browser with optimised print stylesheets.
Barcode & QR Code Generation Built-In
Automatic barcode and QR code generation for all assets, tickets, food supply items, and recipes. Batch barcode printing dialog for bulk label generation. Barcode scanner integration using device camera (HTML5) compatible with all handheld devices.
Excel Import & Export (All Modules) Built-In
Bidirectional Excel (.xlsx) data exchange across all platform modules. Bulk asset import from existing spreadsheets, consumption report exports, stocktaking plan exports, handheld session exports, and SLA compliance reports. Field mapping wizard for legacy data import.
Microsoft Outlook Add-In Built-In
Native Office 365 Outlook add-in enabling technicians and users to create support tickets directly from email context without leaving Outlook. Auto-populates ticket fields from email subject and body. View ticket status, update tickets, and access the asset portal from within Outlook.
Email-to-Ticket Automation (Gmail & Outlook) Built-In
Automatic ticket creation from incoming emails via webhook integration with Gmail, Outlook/Office 365, Mailgun, SendGrid, and any SMTP provider. AI classifies ticket priority and category from email content. Sends auto-acknowledgment to the sender with ticket reference number.
Task Planner with Calendar & KPI Dashboard Built-In
Full task planning module with calendar view, list view, and KPI dashboard. Tasks linked to assets, assigned to team members, with estimated/actual hours tracking, priority levels, status workflow, and AI-suggested task recommendations. Export planned tasks to Excel.
Self-Service Request Portal Built-In
End-user self-service portal with service catalogue-style interface. Users browse service categories, submit requests, track request status, view assigned assets, and access knowledge base. Separate from the admin interface — designed for the 900 QRCS end-users.
Multi-Organisation & Subscription Management Built-In
Full multi-tenant architecture supporting multiple QRCS organisational units. Organisation switching, member management, subscription plan management with usage metrics, license key management, billing history tracking, and org-level data isolation for each department or branch.
Staff Activity & Performance Monitoring Built-In
Real-time staff activity viewer showing technician actions, ticket resolutions, asset operations, and scan activities. Performance metrics per staff member: ticket closure rates, response times, SLA compliance, handheld usage, and comparative team performance dashboards.
Error Logging & System Diagnostics Built-In
Comprehensive error logging and diagnostics panel for system administrators. AI-assisted error analysis with suggested solutions, error trend tracking, severity classification, resolution workflow, and system health monitoring. Supports proactive issue resolution before users are affected.
Bilingual Interface — Arabic & English (RTL/LTR) Built-In
Full Arabic and English language support with complete Right-to-Left (RTL) layout switching. All UI components, reports, alerts, and menus are fully localised. The Translation Editor allows administrators to customise any text string without developer involvement.
Dark Mode & Theme Customisation Built-In
Full dark mode and light mode support across the entire platform including all dashboards, dialogs, and reports. Theme persists per user. Dark mode reduces eye strain for 24/7 operations centre staff and is optimised for field environments with varying lighting conditions.
React Native Mobile App (Android & iOS) Built-In
Fully built Expo React Native mobile app for Android and Apple iOS. Features: Azure AD SSO login, assigned asset management, ticket creation with photo attachment, RFID/barcode scanning via camera, offline SQLite queue with auto-sync, push notifications, and biometric re-authentication. Ready for App Store / Play Store publication.
SAP / ServiceNow / Third-Party Integration On Request
Integration connectors for SAP ERP, ServiceNow ITSM, and any other enterprise platform beyond Microsoft Dynamics 365. Bidirectional sync for assets, work orders, procurement, and incident management. Available as a customisation within the 15% free-of-charge envelope.
SMS & WhatsApp Business Notifications On Request
Real-time SMS and WhatsApp Business API alerts for critical events: SLA breaches, RFID tamper alarms, overdue asset returns, and approval requests. Ensures field staff without email access are immediately notified. Available via Twilio or WhatsApp Business API integration.
Blockchain Asset Provenance On Request
Immutable blockchain-based chain of custody for high-value medical and critical humanitarian assets. Every lifecycle event is cryptographically recorded, providing tamper-proof provenance documentation for donor reporting, audits, and regulatory compliance.
