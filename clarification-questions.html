  • Does QRCS have existing server hardware available, or must the vendor supply it?
  • What operating system is available/preferred (Windows Server / Linux)?

  • Is a warm/hot standby server required?

  • Are backup, DR, and failover requirements in scope for the vendor, or managed by QRCS IT?
Critical
B3
Network Connectivity Between the 7 Sites
  • Are all 7 sites connected via a shared WAN, MPLS, or VPN?
  • What is the available bandwidth between sites and to the central server?
  • Must each site support local offline operation if the WAN link fails, with automatic sync on reconnection?
High
B4
Data Residency, Privacy & Security Compliance
  • Are there data sovereignty or local data residency requirements (data must remain in a specific country)?
  • Is ISO 27001 certification required of the vendor?

High

Performance Benchmarks

RFID Hardware & Infrastructure

10 questions
C1
Existing RFID Infrastructure — Full Specification Required
Section 2.1 states QRCS "already has RFID infrastructure." We must understand this fully before sourcing compatible hardware:
  • Brand and model of existing RFID readers/gateways
  • RFID frequency band in operation (433 MHz, 868 MHz, 915 MHz, 2.4 GHz, or HF 13.56 MHz)
  • Communication protocol used by readers (MQTT, REST API, proprietary?)
  • Are existing readers staying in service, or being replaced as part of this project?
  • Is an SDK or API available for the existing reader infrastructure?
⚠️ Urgent: Our primary RFID hardware supplier in China is not responding. Without existing infrastructure details, we cannot qualify alternative suppliers and guarantee tag/reader compatibility. This may affect our ability to submit a compliant bid.
Critical
C2
Active RFID Tags — 5,000 Tags Specification
  • Required RFID frequency for active tags (433 MHz or 2.4 GHz)?
  • Required read range (meters)?
  • Environmental conditions: indoor / outdoor, temperature range, humidity, chemical exposure?
  • Mounting surface types: metal assets, plastic, glass, composite?
  • Must tags be IP-rated (e.g., IP67/IP68) or MIL-STD rated?
  • Is two-way write capability required or read-only?
Critical
C3
Passive RFID Tags — 10,000 Tags Specification
  • Required RFID standard for passive tags: EPC Gen 2 UHF (860–960 MHz) or HF ISO 15693 / ISO 14443?
  • Required read range (cm or meters)?
  • Are on-metal (in-metal) tags required for metal-surfaced assets?
  • Are standard adhesive label tags acceptable for non-metal assets?
  • Should tags arrive pre-encoded (EPC programmed), or will encoding be performed on-site during deployment?
Critical
C4
Passageway Readers — 7 Sites — Site Survey Access
Section 1.7 requires passageway access control readers at the entrances and exits of all 7 sites and requests a site survey to determine reader quantities. We require:
  • Names, addresses, and approximate sizes of all 7 sites
  • Approximate number of entry/exit passageways per site (for a preliminary estimate)
  • Are passageway readers required for active RFID, passive RFID, or both?
  • Is integration with physical access control (door locks, barriers, turnstiles) required, or monitoring/alerting only?
  • Will QRCS facilitate site visits before bid submission, or is a desktop/virtual survey acceptable?
  • Who is responsible for civil works (cabling, conduit, power supply to readers) — QRCS or the vendor?
Critical
C5
Handheld Devices — 15 Units
  • Should the vendor supply the handheld hardware, or provide software-only integration with QRCS-owned devices?
  • Preferred OS: Android or Windows?
  • Required RFID frequency per handheld (UHF, HF, or both)?
  • Must the handheld include an integrated barcode / QR scanner?
  • Required ruggedness rating (MIL-STD-810G, IP65, or similar)?
  • Required battery life per shift (hours)?
Critical
C6
Active Tag Removal / Anti-Tamper Alarm (Section 1.6)
Section 1.6 requires an alarm when an active RFID tag is physically removed from an asset. This requires anti-tamper sensors on the tag hardware itself.
  • Is anti-tamper alarm capability a mandatory requirement or optional enhancement?
  • Does the existing active tag infrastructure already support anti-tamper alerts?
  • If new anti-tamper tags are required, is the additional cost acceptable?
High
C7
Low Battery Alerts for Active Tags
  • What is the required battery alert threshold (e.g., below 20% or 15%)?
  • Should battery status be transmitted automatically at a set frequency, or only reported on next scheduled RFID scan?
  • Should low battery alerts trigger a maintenance ticket automatically in the system?
High
C8
Electronic Floor Map — Floor Plans Availability (Section 1.5)
Sections 1.5, 1.6, and 2.4 reference zone-based asset tracking displayed on an electronic map.
  • Are digital floor plans (DWG, SVG, PDF, or image format) available for all 7 sites?
  • What level of location accuracy is expected — zone/room-level (RFID zone), or sub-meter precision (requiring a dedicated indoor positioning system)?
  • Is a full Indoor Positioning System (IPS) with location engines in scope, or is RFID zone approximation sufficient?
  • Note: Electronic map customization is marked "extensible" in the RFQ — is a basic zone map acceptable for base scope, with IPS as an optional future extension?
High
C9
RFID Compatibility Standards
Section 2.1 references compatibility with "common RFID standards (e.g., EPC Gen2, ISO standards)." Are there specific certification marks or standards required (e.g., ETSI EN 302 208, FCC Part 15, ISO 18000-63)? Are there any country-specific radio regulations that apply at the 7 sites?
Medium
C10
Asset Flow Path & Distribution Statistics (Section 1.5)
Sections 1.5 marks "Asset flow path data statistics" and "Asset area distribution statistics" as extensible / requiring customization. Should these be included in the base scope, priced as optional additions, or excluded entirely from this bid?
Medium
