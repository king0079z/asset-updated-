Login & Authentication
Sign in with your QRCS email or Microsoft Azure AD account

Option A — Microsoft Azure AD (Recommended)

1
Open the platform
Go to assetxai.live in your browser
2
Click "Sign in with Microsoft"
Click the blue Microsoft button on the login page
3
Enter your QRCS Microsoft credentials
Use your QRCS email address (e.g., name@qrcs.org.qa) and Microsoft password. If MFA is enabled, approve the request on your phone.
4
You're in!
You will be redirected to your personalized portal dashboard automatically.

Option B — Email & Password

1
Enter your email address
Type your QRCS email address in the Email field
2
Enter your password
Type your password. Click the eye icon to show/hide it.
3
Click "Sign In"
Press the Sign In button or hit Enter on your keyboard
Forgot your password? Click "Forgot password?" on the login page and enter your email. You will receive a reset link within 5 minutes.
Account Pending? New accounts require administrator approval. If you see a "Pending Approval" screen, contact your IT administrator or call +974 5560 8507.

Changing Your Password

Go to Settings → Your Profile → Change Password. Enter your current password, then your new password twice. Passwords must be at least 8 characters.

Asset Management
Register, track, assign, and manage all QRCS assets

Registering a New Asset

1
Go to Assets
Click "Assets" in the left sidebar
2
Click "+ New Asset" button
Top-right corner of the assets page
3
Fill in asset details
Name, type, serial number, purchase date, cost, location, and assigned user
4
Attach RFID tag (optional)
Scan the RFID tag ID or enter it manually to link the tag to this asset
5
Save
Click Save. An asset ID and barcode are generated automatically.

Bulk Import from Excel

1
Go to Admin → Data Migration
Access the data migration wizard from the sidebar
2
Download the template
Click "Download Template" for Assets to get the correct Excel format
3
Fill in the template
Add your asset data. Do not change column headers.
4
Upload and run Dry Run first
Upload the file, enable "Dry Run" to preview errors before committing data
5
Uncheck Dry Run and Import
Once the dry run passes, uncheck Dry Run and click Import

Assigning an Asset to a User

Open the asset → Click "Assign" → Search for the user by name or email → Set assignment date → Save. The user receives an email notification and the asset status changes to "Active".

Borrowing & Returns
Track temporary asset loans with automatic overdue alerts

Borrowing an Asset

1
Go to Borrowing & Returns
Click "Borrowing & Returns" in the Assets section of the sidebar
2
Open the Asset
Search for the asset you want to borrow from the Assets page, then click it
3
Fill borrow details
Specify the borrower name/email, borrow location, expected return date, and any notes
4
Confirm
Click "Borrow". The asset status changes to "Borrowed" and the borrower receives a confirmation email.

Returning an Asset

Go to Borrowing & Returns → Find the borrow record → Click "Return" → Confirm. The asset status changes to "Returned" and the record is closed.

Overdue Alerts: If an asset is not returned by its expected return date, the system automatically sends an email reminder to the borrower and marks the record as "Overdue". The borrowing administrator is also notified.

Disposing of an Asset

1
Open the asset

2
Click "Dispose"

Creating an Inspection Route

2

Spare Parts & Service Contracts
Track inventory of parts and manage vendor service agreements

Adding a Spare Part

Go to Spare Parts (Assets section) → Click "+ Add Part" → Enter the part name, part number, quantity, unit cost, reorder level, and storage location → Save.

Low Stock Alert: When a part's quantity falls to or below its reorder level, the system automatically flags it in orange and sends a daily alert email to administrators. Reorder immediately to avoid maintenance delays.

Using a Spare Part Against a Ticket

When resolving a maintenance ticket, go to the ticket → Click the "Use Parts" button → Select the part and quantity used → Confirm. The inventory is reduced automatically and the usage is recorded in the ticket history.

Service Contracts

Go to Service Contracts (Assets section) → "+ New Contract" → Fill in the title, vendor, start/end dates, coverage details, and cost. Set "Notify Days Before Expiry" to receive email alerts ahead of contract renewal deadlines.

Approval Requests
Review and action pending approvals for assets and tickets

Certain actions (asset disposal, borrowing of critical assets, high-value requests) require manager approval before they are processed.

Viewing Pending Approvals

Go to Approval Requests in the Support section of the sidebar. You will see all requests assigned to you, with the requester, request type, and reference details.

Approving or Rejecting

Click the request → Review the details → Click "Approve" or "Reject". If rejecting, a reason is required. The requester is notified by email immediately.

Email Reminders: If you have a pending approval that has not been actioned for 24 hours, the system automatically sends you an email reminder. Approvals pending for more than 48 hours are escalated to your manager.

Linking a Tag to an Asset

1
Go to RFID → Tags
Find the unassigned tag (status: Unassigned) by its Tag ID
2
Click "Assign to Asset"
Search for the target asset and confirm the binding. The tag is now linked 1-to-1.
3
Attach the physical tag
Physically attach the RFID tag to the asset using tamper-evident adhesive
Tag Removal Alert: Once a tag is physically attached and active in the system, any unauthorized removal triggers an immediate alarm. Only remove tags after logging an authorized removal request in the system.

Handheld Devices
Field scanning, offline mode, and bulk operations

Starting a Handheld Session

1
Open the browser on the handheld device
Navigate to assetxai.live and log in with your credentials
2
Go to Assets → Handheld Mode
The platform detects handheld devices and switches to the optimized handheld interface automatically
3
Start scanning
Point the built-in camera or external scanner at a barcode, QR code, or RFID tag. The asset details appear instantly.
4
Perform actions
From the scan result, you can: Create a ticket · Assign the asset · Move it · Check history · Update status

Offline Mode

If your handheld device loses internet connectivity in the field, AssetXAI continues to work offline. All scans and actions are queued locally and automatically synchronized as soon as connectivity is restored. No data is ever lost.

Connectivity Indicator: A green dot in the top-right means online. A yellow dot means offline — your actions are being queued. A red dot means an error — check your WiFi settings.

Passageway Access Control
Monitor asset entry and exit at all 7 QRCS sites

Passageway readers are installed at QRCS site entrances and exits. When an asset carrying an RFID tag passes through, the system automatically records the movement and checks whether it is authorized.

Viewing Passageway Events

Go to Passageways (7 Sites) in the Tracking section. Each site shows its RFID reader status, recent entry/exit events, and any unauthorized movement alerts.

Unauthorized Exit Alert

If an asset exits a site without an authorized transfer record, the system immediately generates an alert, sends an email to the site manager, and creates a ticket for investigation.

RFID Standards: All passageway readers are compatible with EPC Gen2, ISO 18000-6C, and BLE 5.0. The RFID Standards panel (visible in the Passageways page) lists all supported protocols.

Policies & Digital Acknowledgment
Accept asset usage policies and digitally acknowledge asset receipt

Accepting a Policy

When an administrator publishes a new policy requiring your acceptance, you will receive an email notification. Go to Policies & Acknowledgments in the Assets section. Read the policy text and click "Accept Policy". Your acceptance is recorded with a timestamp and your IP address.

Digital Asset Acknowledgment

When you receive a new asset, go to Policies & Acknowledgments → Click "Acknowledge Asset" → Enter the Asset ID (or scan the barcode) → Sign digitally by clicking "Sign & Acknowledge". This creates a permanent record of your receipt.

Policy Gate: If there are policies requiring your acceptance, you will be reminded before you can access certain asset features. Accept all pending policies to unlock full access.
